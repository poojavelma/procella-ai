Yes. We work with the tools you already have — QuickBooks, Clio, Dentrix, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, and many others. We don't ask you to switch platforms.
What does the 30-day guarantee mean exactly? +
If your practice doesn't see measurable, agreed-upon time savings within 30 days of going live with the AI workflows we implement, you don't pay for the implementation. We define the success metrics together upfront — there's no ambiguity.
How much does an implementation typically cost? +
Every engagement is scoped based on your specific practice size and the workflows we're automating. We discuss pricing transparently on the strategy call — typically after reviewing your audit results together. There is no cost for the audit or the strategy call.
Is our client data safe? +
Data security is our highest priority — especially given the sensitive nature of accounting, legal, and medical data. All implementations use enterprise-grade security, and we work within your existing compliance frameworks (HIPAA, attorney-client privilege, etc.).
How is this different from just buying an AI tool off the shelf? +
Off-the-shelf AI tools are generic — they're not configured for your specific workflows, your staff's habits, or your existing software stack. We do the full implementation, customisation, and training. Most practices that try AI tools on their own see limited results because the tool isn't properly integrated into how they actually work.
